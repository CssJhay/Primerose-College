Our Cause

The Trust Council, Management, Parents, Lovers, and the Old Students Association of the above-mentioned schools and college are raising money to complete the following projects in the school for the benefit of students and the community at large.

  • Completion of School hall and furniture ;
  • Laboratory Furniture and modern-day equipment ;
  • Laptops/ desktops for the ICT center;
  • Furniture and books for the library ;
  • Modern-day students' furniture.

    • We need about 20 Million naira (20,000,000.) between now and September 2025 for the above to mark our 30 years of establishment.
